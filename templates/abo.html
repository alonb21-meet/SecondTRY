ABOUT Us

Here at T.R.Y, we're committed to investing our expertise and resources in order to further achieve our cause, violence is spreading more and more in community. we want to raise the future generation on values that will help us building the next non-violent generation. just imagine what we can achieve togather

VISION

We envision a peaceful and tolerant community which
carries a generation that is equipped with the right
tools to resolve conflict in a peaceful way




MISSION

To provide the youth who live in a violent society platform
to meet and develop their sense of belonging through helping
them adopt a more peaceful perspective on striving
for change in the violent community
